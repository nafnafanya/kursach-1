Suddenly, a bright light appears in front of you and you
seem to fall asleep. Dramatically darkness replaces light.
There are many screaming voices around. These were the 
voices of those who are growing in their homes. There was no
rushing wind, but you can feel yourself falling. The further
you fell, the less you heard voices. When they were
completely quiet, someone quietly said:
-You behaved badly! You will wander around this world until
you understand something!
Everything was quiet. You fly for a few more seconds.
Light appears.
